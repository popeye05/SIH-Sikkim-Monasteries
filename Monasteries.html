Monasteries of Sikkim

Discover the sacred sites that form the spiritual bedrock of Sikkim. Each monastery is a living repository of wisdom, art, and history.

Kagyu Lineage • East Sikkim

Rumtek Monastery

The seat of the Karmapa, a world-renowned center for Kagyu teachings and Tibetan Buddhist culture.

Nyingma Lineage • West Sikkim

Pemayangtse Monastery

One of the oldest and most premier monasteries of the Nyingma order, known for its intricate sculptures.

Nyingma Lineage • West Sikkim

Tashiding Monastery

Considered the most sacred monastery, located on a heart-shaped hill with panoramic Himalayan views.

Nyingma Lineage • East Sikkim

Enchey Monastery

A 200-year-old monastery, important to the Nyingma order, perched on a ridge above Gangtok.

Kagyu Lineage • North Sikkim

Phodong Monastery

One of the six major monasteries in Sikkim, known for its beautiful original murals and frescoes.

Kagyu Lineage • South Sikkim

Ralang Monastery

Famous for its impressive collection of paintings and thangkas, and for hosting the Pang Lhabsol festival.
