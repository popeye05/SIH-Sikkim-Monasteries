Our Sacred Mission

The Monastic Education and Spiritual Worship Organization (MESWO) is dedicated to the preservation, promotion, and propagation of Sikkim's profound Buddhist heritage. We serve as a central body to support our monasteries, archive ancient wisdom, and share the timeless practice of meditation with the world.

Featured Monastery: Rumtek

A Beacon of the Karma Kagyu Lineage

Perched on a hill facing Gangtok, Rumtek Monastery is one of the most significant centers of Tibetan Buddhism in the world. Originally built in the 16th century, it was re-established by the 16th Karmapa in the 1960s to preserve the teachings of his lineage.

Today, it stands as a vibrant hub of monastic life, education, and practice, housing sacred relics and hosting elaborate ceremonies that draw devotees from across the globe.

From the Dharma Treasury

The Essence of Bodhicitta

Delve into a profound teaching on Bodhicitta, the compassionate aspiration for enlightenment for the benefit of all beings. This foundational Mahayana concept is the very heart of the Bodhisattva path. Learn about its two aspects—aspirational and practical—and discover methods to cultivate this boundless mind.

A Word of Wisdom

"The mind is like a clear sky. Clouds, which are thoughts and emotions, come and go, but the sky itself remains unstained and limitless. To meditate is to rest as the sky."

— A Teaching from a Sikkim Lama
