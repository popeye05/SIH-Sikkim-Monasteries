Preserve a Legacy, Support the Sangha

Your generosity helps us protect and share the timeless wisdom of Sikkim's monastic traditions with the world.

How Your Gift Helps

MESWO is dedicated to the preservation and promotion of Sikkim's invaluable spiritual heritage. Contributions from compassionate individuals like you are the lifeblood of our work. Your support ensures that ancient monasteries stand strong, that precious texts are archived for future generations, and that the profound teachings of the Dharma continue to inspire peace and wisdom.

Monastery Restoration

Help us maintain and restore sacred temples, stupas, and murals that are centuries old.

Education & Archives

Support the education of young monks and nuns, and help us digitize and preserve ancient manuscripts.

Community Support

Provide for the basic needs of the monastic community, including food, healthcare, and utilities.
